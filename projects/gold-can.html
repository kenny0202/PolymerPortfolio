The Golden Can

The Golden Can was a website that I have developed along with my team for the Introduction to web development course. We had many different ideas in mind such as websites similar to urbanspoon and yelp, craigslist and kijiji, but ultimately we wanted to create something unique that has rarely been done before. We came up with the idea of creating a restaurant wash room rating website.

The Golden Can allowed users to register for an account. Once they have an account they are able to submit reviews and users who access the websites are able to read the review of the wash room and view the pictures posted. Users can also post comments on a wash room review.

The website was developed using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL.
